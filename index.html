Household Plants

There are many household plants which can provide a decorative and warm touch to your home. Below are a few examples of such plants, so you can pick the right one for you.

Peace Lily (Spathiphyllum wallisii) The peace lily has exceptional qualities, its lance-shaped leaves emanate a tropical feel, even when indoors.

Rubber Plant (Ficus elastica) The rubber plant is great for indoor decoration, its dark green appearance gives it a wonderful forest-like aspect. It also thrives in high-humidity, making it a perfect choice for a summer plant.

Pothos (Epipremnum pinnatum) The pathos plant is an additionally highly-desirable household plant, its low maintenance and high resilience in low-light conditions makes it a staple household item.
